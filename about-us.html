Short introduction about

Kapil & Mmonika

It is a rare affair when two gifted individuals with such ingenious talent get an opportunity to not only spend their life together, but also work in sync. Kapil&mmonika launched their eponymous label in year 2006. When they realized serious lacunae in the category of ‘customized couture’. Kapil&mmonika has a wide range of creativity. However, femininity forms the essence of the label with styles that signify youth, glamour and sophistication. Inspired by anything beautiful, they always had an extremely romantic design philosophy with styles that are luxurious yet understated.

Mission & Vision

Their garments applaud a strong emphasis on fabrics, cuts & colours, the designs are all about shape and flattering silhouettes. By combining tailoring with drapery, the garments are meticulously crafted and have a timeless appeal where old world charm, fantasy and elegance are brought bang up to date.

The self-trained designer duo with a keen eye for aesthetics and interesting interpretation of traditional Indian craftsmanship. Kapil&mmonika uses exclusive fabrics like velvet, georgette silk with right blend of rich heritage textile with delicate blend of western cuts, layers, are adorned with embellishments like swarovaski crystal, pearls, with detailed art and craft technique.

Our Promise

After specialization in Couture & Bridal Wear they launched a range of luxury pret’ collection to accommodate the wider audience now K&M can be worn on not only hi- end parties or weddings but also for the corporate meeting and lunches. They have remained true to the consistency of their designs and quality of their product throughout their tenure in the fashion industry and are quite upbeat with their collection.

They witnessed the grandeur and beauty of fashion and cherished it with bollywood stars and élites. since the time it commenced, they have been associated with many national and international fashion shows. Kapil&mmonika got honored by Indian embassy of Oman by representing Indian fashion to royal family of Oman. Their designs received such an over whelming response from buyers all over the country that even the who’s who of the celebrities world was dressed in Kapil&mmonika!

They cater to an endless list of well-known clientele which includes Bollywood stars, socialites, politicians and sportspersons, amongst others. Bollwood divas like Madhuri Dixit, Karishma Kapoor, Diana Hayden, Prachi Desai, Mahi Gill, Mughda Godse, Madhur Bhandarkar, Rohit Roy, Jackey Bhagnani, Kapil Dev and many more have caught the attention of the media in their outfits.

The Flagship Store- Kapil&mmonika flagship store in the heart of Delhi where it allows fashionistas choose among the best of men’s, women’s wear & children’s wear
